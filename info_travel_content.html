Travel Information

The most direct means of travel is Limoges International Airport which links to Stansted, Southampton, Liverpool and Midlands airports, flights through Ryan Air and flybe. Booking in advance does make it cheaper. We can collect people from the airport or you can hire a car there. Our base is about 35 minutes drive from the airport - not on busy roads.
Alternatively you can drive from the UK, which takes about 8 hours from the Dunkirk/Calais/Boulogne ports. (Dunkirk have some good offers on at the moment too) You can get maps and driving directions from the following sites:
  • Google Maps
  • Mappy
It is also possible to come by train. You can cross on the Eurostar to Paris and get the train to Limoges, where we can collect you at the station.
