Non-Painting Partners

Non painting partners are made very welcome. They can relax in the grounds of the hotel or there are numerous activities for them to follow, from golf to flying. In the evenings they will rejoin the artists as the focus shifts to the ‘art of the table’ and convivial conversation. The painting excursions, of course, can be enjoyed by everyone.

Non painting activities

  • Classic Car Hire – Various classic cars available.
  • Walking – The hotel is surrounded by beautiful countryside with many marked trails to choose from.
  • Golf – There is a very good, pay and play, 18 hole golf course at St Junien.
  • Flying – lessons are available at very reasonable rates.
  • Horse riding – there are a number of stables close by.
  • French – Improve your French language skills.
  • Cycling – Road, off-road and ‘Velo-rail’.
  • Tennis – There are courts close by.
  • Canoeing – Several centres in the area.
