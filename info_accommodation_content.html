Accommodation Information

Our base is a delightful hotel run by Jean-Pierre and Chantal Teycheny. All rooms have en suite facilities and are individually themed providing both sleeping and relaxing areas.
Breakfast, lunch and evening meals are included, the experience ‘à la table’ offers an important contributing element to the ‘house party’ ambiance of an Art Barn painting holiday.
Happily Chantal is a superb chef specialising in regional cuisine. She will also cater for vegetarians and special diets on request.
