Painters will need to provide their own art materials.
The studio, equipped with boards, easels and work tables, is available to artists at any time. Some equipment will be available for purchase at cost price.

Painters are advised to bring:-

  • A selection of drawing pencils
  • Sketchbook
  • Watercolour block
  • Paint box – pans or tubes
  • A selection of paint brushes

A complete list of equipment will be recommended in the Course Information Pack
