About the course

Our aim is to accommodate painters of all standards. We especially welcome beginners - we believe everyone should be encouraged to find the artist within and to discover the joys of creating art. We have chosen to concentrate on watercolour as, we think, it is the perfect medium for painting holiday courses – not least because the equipment is extremely compact, portable and affordable.
